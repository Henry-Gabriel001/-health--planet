EAR PAIN

The following 4steps may help an ear ache:
1.Place a cold pack or cold wet washcloth on the outer ear for 20mins to reduce pain .
2.Chewing may help relieve the pain and pressure of an ear infection.
3.Resting in an upright position instead of lying down can reduce pressure in the middle ear.
4.Take diclofenac tab, put chlorophenicol eyedrop and sleep.
